The Container Analysis Environments workshop was held August 14-16, 2017, at the National Center for Supercomputing Applications (NCSA) on the University of Illinois campus and brought together researchers and cyberinfracture developers involved in the development and use of systems that leverage container based platforms to facilitate access to research data and research computing infrastructure. The workshop was supported by the NDS and in cooperation with the Data Exploration Lab. The workshop report is now available on Zenodo.
