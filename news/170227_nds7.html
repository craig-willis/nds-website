The seventh National Data Service Consortium workshop (NDS7) will be held in Chicago on April 13-14, 2017. The workshop's theme is "Scientific Data Platforms: from Silos to Ecosystems."

The meeting will be located at the Hotel Allegro, a boutique hotel in the heart of downtown Chicago. We have obtained a special discounted rate for accommodation at the hotel.

NDS7 seeks to attract participation from a broad community including researchers, developers, librarians, publishers, and others. The NDS builds on the data archiving and sharing efforts already underway within specific communities and links them together with a common set of tools designed to facilitate discovery and re-use of research data.

NDS7 will be co-located with GlobusWorld 2017 which will be hosted on the two days prior to NDS7. GlobusWorld 2017 is focused on "Building the Research Applications and Services Ecosystem." Co-location of NDS and GlobusWorld will enable participants to attend both meetings and facilitate the exchange of ideas between these two overlapping communities. A joint poster session and reception will be held on Wednesday evening.
