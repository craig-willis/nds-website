The easiest way to contact the NDS project is to send mail to info@nationaldataservice.org, join us on Slack , or submit your query via the form below.

NDS activities are coordinated via teams at the National Center for Supercomputing Applications at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and the San Diego Supercomputing Center at the University of California San Diego .
