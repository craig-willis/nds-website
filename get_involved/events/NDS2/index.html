2nd National Data Service Consortium Workshop

  • October 23-24, 2014
  • Rockville, Maryland

The second National Data Service (NDS) Consortium Planning Workshop will be held Oct. 23-24, 2014, at the Hilton Washington DC/Rockville Hotel & Executive Meeting Center in Rockville, Maryland. During this meeting, we will continue the discussions from our last meeting on how the NDS can revolutionize science through sharing and publishing data, what the NDS should look like, how we can build it, and more.
