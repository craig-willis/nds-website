3rd National Data Service Consortium Workshop

  • March 25-27, 2015
  • Austin, Texas

Join us in Austin where we will focus on establishing the governance for the NDS Consortium, growing the pilot projects, and sharing the latest on funding efforts. The first day, March 25, will be Pilots Day, a chance for pilot project participants to meet, develop, and plan for on-going activity. The plenary program starts on the second day, March 26. To receive register for the meeting, please request an invitation via email to info@nationaldataservice.org.
