Wednesday, October 19

Wednesday October 19, will feature a special tutorial for NDS Labs developers. In the morning, we will provide an introduction to NDS Labs and a session on getting started with Docker . In the afternoon, we will go over the process of adding a new component in the NDS Labs environment to make it available as a service, deploying and connecting tools to build new resources, and how to get started with developing against the available technologies. Please watch our video to learn more about NDS Labs.

Thursday, October 20

The plenary program starts on the second day, October 20 and our theme will be New Frontiers in Data Discovery: Collaboration with Research Libraries. The organizing committee is now contacting leaders in the library community for plenary presentations. Plenary talks will be on topics including but not limited to data, data services, metadata and interoperability. As speakers are confirmed we will update the meeting website.
