There are several ways to interact with NDS members: Subscribe to our Slack channel and our mailing list. To join the mailing list send an email to majordomo@nationaldataservice.org with the text "subscribe nds-announce" in the body of the message.

To unsubscribe from the mailing list send an email to majordomo@nationaldataservice.org with the text "unsubscribe nds-announce" as the body of the message. If unsubscribing from a different address then the one you subscribed with, send the text "unsubscribe nds-announce your_email" instead, where your_email is the address you subscribed with.

Notes:

  • Messages sent to-date can be found in the list archive
  • Members may not post to this list; for questions regarding this list, contact us at info@nationaldataservice.org
