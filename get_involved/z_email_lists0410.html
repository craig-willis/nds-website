There are several ways to interact with NDS members: Subscribe to our Slack channel and mailing lists. To join the mailing lists, enter your email address into the appropriate forms below or send an email to majordomo@nationaldataservice.org with the text "subscribe listname" in the body of the message, where listname is the name of the list.

The public lists are:

  • nds-announce
  • discuss
  • consortium

Receive news and updates about NDS activities

List name: nds-announce@nationaldataservice.org

We use this list to keep people interested in NDS up-to-date on its latest activities.

To subscribe:

To unsubscribe:
Send an email to majordomo@nationaldataservice.org with the text "unsubscribe nds-announce" as the body of the message. If unsubscribing from a different address then the one you subscribed with, send the text "unsubscribe nds-announce your_email" instead, where your_email is the address you subscribed with.

Notes:

  • Messages sent to-date can be found in the list archive
  • Members may not post to this list; for questions regarding this list, contact us at info@nationaldataservice.org
