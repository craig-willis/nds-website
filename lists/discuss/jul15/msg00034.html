Over the weekend, I rebuilt, tested, and redeployed all of the NDS services to DockerHub. They are all running in the "coreos_mfreemon" NDS Labs Environment on the OpenStack cluster at NCSA.

These include:

curldrop
rabbitmq-server
hublaunch
irods-icat
postgres-icat
irods-idrop2
irods-rest
kallithea
moinmoin
owncloud
postgres-owncloud
rstudio
proxy
nginx

The various adjustments and fixes are:
https://bitbucket.org/nds-org/nds-labs/commits/765eac1fef364f24ca8db99000bb39a39c8579b6

A refresher about what these services do can be found here:
https://wiki.ncsa.illinois.edu/display/NDS/NDS+Services

These services are available from either DockerHub in the form of a downloadable docker image, or from the source code repository in Bitbucket.

https://registry.hub.docker.com/repos/ndslabs/
https://bitbucket.org/nds-org/nds-labs

Please report problems or send along any suggestions for enhancements or improvement you might have.

Contributions to the source are always encouraged and are most welcome. This includes new services that do not yet exist.

If you have any questions, let me know.  Thank you.

- Mike
