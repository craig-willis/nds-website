Dataverse 4.0 is here!


Harvard Dataverse has been upgraded to Dataverse 4.0 and is available for all to use at dataverse.harvard.edu!

Please note that you will have to enter a new password when logging in for the first time due to stricter password requirements. Passwords in 4.0 must be at least 6 characters and contain at least one number and letter.

Thank you to all the Dataverse users who participated in usability testing and provided feedback. Dataverse 4.0 would not be the same without you!
