Hi NDS friends,

We expect that we'll see many of you at Research Data Alliance 
Plenary 5 meeting in San Diego (3/8-11/2015, www.rd-alliance.org).  We 
do want to call your attention to a special session that is 
particularly relevent to the NDS.

The Large-scale Data Projects Session (on Sat. 3/8) will address how 
to advance the goals of RDA and further increase adoption with tool 
developers and researchers in the larger context of large data 
facilities and projects.  A call for papers to present during 
this session is now open; the deadline to submit is Feb. 8.  

hope to see you there,
