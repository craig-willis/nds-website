Are you planning to run a workshop, tutorial, or hackathon and need a scalable solution to provide fast, web-base access to specialized software environments? The Labs Workbench platform has been effectively used to support a variety of hackathon and training environments including the Einstein Toolkit School, PI4 Data Science Bootcamp and Think Chicago Civic Tech Challenge. Hosting services are available at SDSC, NCSA and TACC via the NDS consortium. Hosting workbench requires completion and approval of a pilot project application.

See NDS Labs Workbench for more information about the platform and features.
